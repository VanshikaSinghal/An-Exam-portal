Directions for the candidates

  1. Examination Hall will be opened 15 minutes before the time specified for the commencement of the Examination. No candidate will be allowed after the commencement of Exam.
  2. Candidate should find their place as per the seating plan and be seated before the Exams commencement.
  3. Candidates should maintain silence and discipline during Exams as expected and should not leave the exam room/Hall without handing over the OMR Answer sheet.
  4. Candidates should blacken properly the answer circles and other details in the OMR with Blue/black Ball point pen. Be sure of your choice before blackening your answer and other details. Corrections should be strictly avoided.OMR sheet should not be folded .
  5. No candidate will be allowed to sit in the Exams without the emailed Admit card.Ensure that your photograph has been attested. Carrying an Identity Proof of yourself is advised.
  6. Mobiles/Electronic gadgets are not allowed inside the Exam Hall/Room.
  7. Giving or taking assistance directly or indirectly will be treated as Unfair Means(UFM).
  8. Centre Superintendents decision will be final on UFMs and candidates found guilty will be debarred for 5 years from appearing in STET.
  9. All passed candidates will be issued certificates only after the final scrutiny of their documents/marksheets submitted in hard copy.
  10. This Admit Card is issued only to those candidates who submitted their forms online & also the Hard-copy at HRDD for verification and authenticity.
