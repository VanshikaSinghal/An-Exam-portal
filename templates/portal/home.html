Introduction

(Last updated:12/10/19)

STET Sikkim Exam is a Teacher Eligibility Test conducted by The Human Resource Development Department of Sikkim (HRDD). Sikkim has stood 3rd in the national ranking on elementary education for the year 2013-14 as per flash statistics released by Smt. Smriti Irani Hon’ble Union HRD Minister on 18.6.2014. The ranking is based on the four parameters of Accessibility, Infrastructure, Teacher and Outcome. Candidates should first check the Exam Eligibility Criteria before applying for the exam. It’s a very good opportunity for Sikkim State candidates to work in such a very good Education system.

An applicant who intends to participate in both the categories (primary and Graduate) will have to apply and sit in both the papers separately and pay the fee for both the tests. Both Level I and Level II (i.e. for Primary and Graduate teacher) will be conducted on the same day. Total number of questions will be 150 containing Multiple Choice Questions. For each Correct Answer 1 marks will be awarded and there will be no negative marking in STET Sikkim Exam 2019. Candidates will be given 2.30 hours for solving full Question paper. The Exam Timings are 10.00 am to 12:30 pm for (Elementary (GT) teacher) and 01:30 pm to 4.00 pm for (Primary teacher).
